✦ Features

Everything you need to manage the family budget

A complete yet simple tool, designed for real everyday life.

📊

Charts & Reports

Analyze income and expenses with interactive monthly charts and discover exactly where your money goes.

  • Monthly bar charts with account and category filters
  • 90-day balance projection
  • CSV and PDF data export (Premium)
☁️

Real-time Cloud Sync

Data syncs instantly across all household devices. Always up to date, wherever you are.

  • Real-time updates without reloading
  • Available in the Premium Cloud plan
  • Offline mode: the app works without internet
👨‍👩‍👧

Family Multi-user

Invite family members and manage the budget together, each from their own smartphone.

  • Up to 10 members in Premium Cloud plan
  • Each member adds expenses in real time
  • Shared view of accounts and transactions
💳

Accounts & Credit Cards

Keep track of all your financial tools in one place, with always up-to-date balances.

  • Current accounts, cash, savings accounts
  • Credit cards with statement and billing dates
  • Balance adjustable with opening date
🎯

Savings Goals

Set financial milestones and monitor progress over time, contribution by contribution.

  • Holidays, purchases, emergency funds
  • Manual contributions with history
  • Visual progress bar with completion %
🔁

Recurring Transactions

Subscriptions, salaries and rent generate automatically. Never miss an expense again.

  • Automatic generation at due date
  • Installment support with payment plans
  • Up to 4 recurring in Free plan, unlimited in Premium
🏦

Monthly Budgets

Set a budget for each category and monitor in real time how much you have already spent against the limit.

  • Budget per category with overspending alerts
  • Spent vs planned comparison
  • Month-by-month budget history
🔒

Privacy & Security

Your financial data is protected. You can choose to never send it to the cloud if you prefer maximum privacy.

  • App lock with fingerprint or Face ID
  • Local mode: data only on device
  • No financial data shared with third parties
✦ Interface

An interface designed for simplicity

Clean, fast, intuitive. All the important information always at your fingertips.
